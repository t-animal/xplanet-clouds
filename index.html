Cloud image for xplanet

This is mainly for my private use, but feel free to checkout out https://github.com/t-animal/xplanet-clouds for my scripts and base images.

Images here are regenerated every 6 hours (at 0:00, 6:00, 12:00 and 18:00 UTC).

Download them using wget -N <image-url> (this only downloads if the image has changed).
